                                   Some Methods:

 Scrum: TIME-Boxed Method with Process and Ceremonies for Agile Software Development
             ( Used in development Projects)


 Kanban: Method to Reduce work in Progress and ensure smooth flow of products in pipeline.
         ( Used in maintenance Projects)


Extreme programming: Defines a collection of Technical practices for quick development of quality software 
which can lend itself to change.

( used along with others flavors of Agile)

Lean : Defines the process of Removal of waste in workflow.

( Used in Manufacturing Environments)
